# 🎬 Timeline Notebook Plus (v5.3)

**Stop using external notes. Direct your animation, manage pose adjustments, and camera cues without ever leaving the 3D scene.**

---

## 📄 Overview
**Timeline Notebook Plus** is a high-end productivity tool for **Blender 5.0+** designed for animators who value flow and organization. It replaces the limited native Marker system with a modern, aesthetic **HUD (Heads-Up Display)**, allowing you to anchor detailed production instructions to specific intervals in your timeline.

---

## ✨ Key Features
*   **Aesthetic HUD:** Modern, semi-transparent interface at the top of your screen.
*   **Interval Support:** Notes appear and disappear based on specific start/end frames.
*   **Quick Jump:** One-click navigation to snap the playhead to any specific note.
*   **Production Categories:** Color-coded tasks for Fixes, Polishing, Camera Cues, and General Info.
*   **Context Filtering:** Display only the notes related to your currently selected object.
*   **Native Sync:** Automatically generates standard Blender markers for timeline reference.

---

## 🛠️ Why Choose Timeline Notebook Plus?

*   **Interruption-Free Workflow**: Stop minimizing Blender to check feedback in PDFs or external note apps.
*   **Production-Ready Communication**: Perfect for lead animators leaving instructions for a team—or for your "future self".
*   **Anti-Overlap Logic**: A built-in safety system ensures your workspace stays clean by preventing overlapping notes in the same slot.
*   **Lightweight & Stable**: Optimized code with **RNA error protection**, ensuring the addon doesn't lag your scene, even in heavy production files.
